FAQ — About Rent & Purchase

🚗 How does car rental work?
Choose a car on the Rent page, fill out the form, and our manager will contact you to confirm details.
💰 Can I buy the car after renting it?
Yes. If you fall in love with the car, you can purchase it after renting — part of your rental payment is deducted.
🧾 What documents do I need to rent a car?
You only need a valid driving license, ID, and a credit/debit card for deposit.
🛠️ What if the car breaks down?
We provide 24/7 support and a free replacement vehicle within a few hours.
💳 How can I pay for buying a car?
Payment options include credit/debit cards, bank transfers, or installment plans for some models.
